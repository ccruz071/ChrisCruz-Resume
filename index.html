My name is Christopher Cruz, and I am a passionate advocate for the intersection of music and psychology. As an undergraduate student, my academic focus is on exploring the fascinating fields of music and psychology. I am currently pursuing a degree in both subjects, driven by my passion for understanding the psychological impact of music. Through my studies, I have gained an in-depth understanding of music theory, history, and composition, as well as the various psychological theories and methods used to study the effects of music on the mind and body. In addition to my academic pursuits, I have also been involved in various extracurricular activities related to music and psychology. For instance, I am an active member of the university's music club, where I collaborate with other students to create and perform original compositions. Furthermore, I have also volunteered at a local mental health clinic, where I witnessed firsthand the therapeutic benefits of music for patients struggling with various mental health issues. This experience has only further solidified my belief in the power of music and its potential to improve people's lives.
